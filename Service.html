Being an TA

Maybe it’s not obvious just how being a teaching assistant can count as service, but I really try to take it the extra mile and work beyond what I get paid to do. I often stay after class with students to help them learn, even though I don’t need to do so. As any teacher will readily tell you, there is so much more going on behind the scenes than you would initially suspect. Even though it’s tough I love giving back to the younger students and helping them on their path.
